Food for a family

Happy meals ..healthy family.. The Happy meals. Happy Families program is aimed at providing families with constant nutritious food supplies to ensure a healthy childhood and bring them to mainstream education to lift the burden of poverty that has plagued them for generations.

Shelter for a family

Family Promise uses a variety of shelter models to ensure that each family that needs us has a place where they can feel safe and supported until they find permanent housing. Rotational shelter is Family Promise's original shelter model.
